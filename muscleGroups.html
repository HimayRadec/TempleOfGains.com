DIFFERENT MUSCLE GROUPS

Chest

The chest is called the pectoral major which consists of the upper and lower chest.

Back

The back consists of the trapezius, latissimus dorsi (known as the lats), rhomboids (upper and lower), and erector spinae.

Shoulders

The shoulder consists of the deltoids. There are three different types: anterior, lateral and rear.

Arms

The biceps consists of brachi, which has a long and a short head. The triceps have a long, lateral and medial head.

Legs

The legs consists of the glutes, the quadriceps (quads), hamstrings, and calves.

Abs

The abs consists of the abdominis and obliques.
